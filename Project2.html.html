Project 2

Research work

My research on gas desulfurization reveals that, while not addressing a novel phenomenon, it underscores limitations in building a gas purifier prototype. Despite successfully desulfurizing gases with a purification tower, the lack of resources prevents a detailed assessment of its performance. Practical conditions and required supervision have limited my ability to explore the effectiveness of alternative reactants. Nevertheless, I have demonstrated that a student can construct a filter to prevent gas release. This work highlights the ease of building a sulfur dioxide purifier and underscores the importance of addressing industrial pollution. This project has provided me with valuable knowledge and skills for future research.
